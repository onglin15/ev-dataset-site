EV Intelligent Driving Dataset

This dataset is developed by our research group for intelligent driving analysis. It includes multimodal data collected from real vehicles.

Data Modalities

  • Multi-camera (front, rear, left, right)
  • GNSS / GPS data
  • Vehicle CAN / OBD signals (SOC, energy consumption)
